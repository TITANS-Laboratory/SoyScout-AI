The Challenge
Farmers Need Better In-Season
Decision Tools
Illinois soybean farmers face growing complexity in managing pests, diseases, nutrients, and weeds — yet no single tool integrates these decisions into a unified, growth-stage framework built for Illinois conditions.

The ISA February 2026 Researcher Roundtable identified a critical gap: farmers need a decision support application that provides actionable, in-field guidance organized by growth stage — showing what to look for and when to act throughout the season.

SoyScout AI addresses this gap with a mobile-friendly tool that combines AI-powered diagnostics, agronomic expertise, and per-acre economic analysis — purpose-built for the unique challenges of Illinois soybean production, from Southern to Northern Illinois.

Core Capabilities
What SoyScout AI Does
Four integrated modules designed around what farmers actually need in the field.
📋
Growth-Stage Scouting Guides
Stage-specific checklists from VE through R8 tell you exactly what to scout for, when action thresholds are reached, and what management options are available — customized for your region of Illinois.
📸
AI-Powered Field Diagnosis
Snap a photo of a suspicious leaf, pest, or weed with your phone. Our machine learning models identify the problem and recommend management actions — trained on Illinois-specific soybean conditions.
💰
Per-Acre Economic Analysis
Every recommendation includes cost-benefit analysis on a per-acre basis. Input your acreage to see projected payback timelines — because management decisions should always pencil out.
🌾
Regional Customization
Disease pressure, soil conditions, and management timing vary across Illinois. SoyScout AI incorporates regional parameters for Southern, Central, and Northern Illinois to provide locally relevant guidance.
